This is a collection of articles that students in Business Ethics tweet. It was created for the people who do not have a Twitter, and it also makes it easier to access all the articles in one place. The articles are pulled from twitter using IFTT and then added to a google spreadsheet which is then put onto this Website. I do not collect any information. I just publish the article link whenever anyone tweets with the #FoxBizEthics.

Here is the Google Spreadsheet, the spreadsheet is open for anyone to edit, you can add to, delete or do anything that you chose, all the changes will be instantly added to the site.

In order to keep the page from becoming over crowded, I will turn each article into PDFs- I will use a reading friendly format that cuts out ads- and I will also add the videos to the folder. Once links are taken down, the articles will be in the folder from the semester that it was added.
